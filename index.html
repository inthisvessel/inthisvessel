I'm a senior Psychology student at Kennesaw State University, transitioning into UX design and product management — and bringing with me years of cross-industry experience in healthcare, banking and operations.

My psychology background isn't a detour. It's my sharpest tool. I understand why people do what they do, what breaks their trust, and what earns it back — and I bring that into every product decision.

When I'm not designing, I'm studying the systems around me — in people, in processes, and in the quiet space between intention and action.

Background

Every role I've held has been about the same thing: seeing who the system leaves behind and figuring out why.

From coordinating patient referrals at a community health clinic, to ensuring dental operations, and to handling fraud calls, I've spent years in the spaces where systems meet real people — and where they fail them. Psychology gave me the framework. Experience gave me the conviction.
